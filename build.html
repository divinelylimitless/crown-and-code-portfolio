About Build Your Future

Build Your Future is a six-week confidence, digital skills and employability programme helping women gain practical skills, build confidence and create new opportunities through technology, AI and entrepreneurship.

Who Is It For?

Single Mothers

Women Returning To Work

Aspiring Entrepreneurs

Women Seeking Digital Skills

Learn Together. Grow Together.

Participants learn practical digital skills, AI tools, personal branding, website creation, LinkedIn, CV development and entrepreneurship in a supportive environment.

  • ✓ Create Opportunities
  • ✓ Grow Your Network

Meet Your Facilitator

Build Your Future is led by Alicia Samuels, founder of The Art of Empowerment CIC and Crown & Code Studio.

Alicia brings together community work, digital skills, creativity and lived experience to help women build confidence, learn practical technology skills and create new opportunities.

Who is the programme for?

Women who want to build confidence, improve digital skills and create new opportunities for work, business or personal growth.

Do I need experience?

No. The programme is beginner-friendly and designed to support women at different confidence levels.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, participants who complete the programme will receive a certificate of completion.
