ABOUT THE TRAINING

Authentic Ving Tsun in East London.

Sifu Chris Samuels has over 40 years of experience in Wing Chun Kung Fu. As a London ambassador for the Wong Shun Leung Ving Tsun lineage — the same system that influenced Bruce Lee under Ip Man — his training is rooted in discipline, structure, practical self-defence, and traditional martial arts wisdom. Through authentic Ving Tsun training, students develop confidence, awareness, discipline, and real-world self-defence skills in a focused and supportive environment.
