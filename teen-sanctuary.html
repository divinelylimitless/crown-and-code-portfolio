✨ First Client Project

Teen Sanctuary by Mothers

Project Overview

Teen Sanctuary by Mothers is a community-led organisation supporting girls, young women and mothers through mentorship, emotional support, guidance and connection.

The Challenge

The client needed a calming online presence that reflected the warmth, care and supportive nature of the organisation while providing a simple way for visitors to learn more and get in touch.

The Solution

Crown & Code Studio designed and developed a responsive landing page using HTML and CSS. The site features soft branding, clear messaging, mobile-friendly design and simple contact pathways.

Project Outcome

The client was happy with the final design and the website now provides a welcoming online space where visitors can learn about the organisation, its mission and how to get in touch.
