ABOUT THE SIFU

Sifu Chris Samuels

Sifu Chris Samuels has over 40 years of experience in Wing Chun Kung Fu. As a London ambassador for the Wong Shun Leung Ving Tsun lineage, his training is rooted in discipline, structure, practical self-defence, and traditional martial arts wisdom.
